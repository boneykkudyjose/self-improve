Title: How to Disagree Without Getting Defensive 

Speaker emphasizes the importance of disagreeing with someone while preventing them from becoming defensive.

Jefferson Fiser mentions that he sees things differently and acknowledges that individuals may have opposing viewpoints. He believes in taking a different approach to understanding various perspectives.

When encountering conflicting opinions, Jefferson Fiser explains that he tends to look at the situation from a different angle based on his experiences. He suggests that individuals can have the same goal but may approach it differently.

He highlights the significance of considering diverse viewpoints and advocates for respectful disagreements without arousing defensiveness. Jefferson Fiser encourages open communication and understanding to prevent conflicts.

In conclusion, Jefferson Fiser emphasizes the value of expressing differing opinions without causing confrontation. By offering alternative perspectives and approaches, individuals can engage in constructive dialogue and learn from each other.
