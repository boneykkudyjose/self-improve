Title: How to Handle Toxic People

The speaker discusses strategies for dealing with toxic individuals. 

Firstly, he emphasizes the importance of doubting their interest in you. It is crucial to pay attention to the level of attention you give them. 

Furthermore, the speaker advises limiting the amount of time you spend with toxic people to protect yourself and send a clear signal. 

In conclusion, he suggests creating distance and setting boundaries to avoid getting drawn into their drama and negative behavior. By doing so, you prioritize your well-being and mental health.
