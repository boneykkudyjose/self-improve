Title: How to Stop Oversharing!

In the video, the speaker discusses the negative aspects of oversharing. They highlight that if you wouldn't share your personal closet with someone, you shouldn't share your personal thoughts with them either. Oversharing can be likened to handing out your favorite sweatshirt to someone else.

Furthermore, the speaker emphasizes that oversharing can lead to undervaluing the significance of your words. They caution that sharing too much about yourself can come at a high cost. Instead, the speaker suggests that creating a sense of mystery can lead to a better perception by others.

Additionally, the video stresses the importance of not talking excessively about oneself. Finally, the speaker concludes that the answer to whether one talks too much should always be 'yes,' encouraging viewers to follow their advice.
