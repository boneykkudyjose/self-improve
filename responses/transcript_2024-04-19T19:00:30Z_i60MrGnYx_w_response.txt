Title: How to Answer a Question with Confidence

In the video, the speaker discusses how to confidently respond to a question. 

They mention that answering a question with the phrase "number one is so easy that anybody can do it" is a good way to build confidence in your response.

Additionally, they suggest replacing uncertain phrases with more assertive ones, such as "I believe" or "I'm confident."

The speaker emphasizes the importance of using phrases that reinforce positive character traits and demonstrate suitability for the role.

They explain that using phrases like "I'm confident" can help to convey expertise and readiness for the position being discussed.

Furthermore, the speaker advises using feedback-seeking phrases like "What are your thoughts?" to encourage open communication and collaboration.

In conclusion, the speaker recommends framing responses with phrases that justify one's experience and position, leaving a strong and confident impression on the listener.
