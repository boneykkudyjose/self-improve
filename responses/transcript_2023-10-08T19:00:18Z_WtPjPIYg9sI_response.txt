Title: Quick Tip on how to Stop an Argument!

In this quick tip, the advice is to stop an argument before it starts, so the next time that somebody snaps at you or says something that is out of line instead of getting defensive and saying excuse me or what did you just say, do this: ask the question, "Did you have a good day?" That's right, this simple question will find out what this person is expressing to you stems from a feeling that was caused long before they even walked into the room.

It turns into a conversation instead of an argument, so try that and follow me!
